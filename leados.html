What's Inside

Everything Your Sales
Team Actually Needs.

🔁

Full Lead-to-Client Pipeline

Every contact flows through a clear journey: New → Contacted → Proposal Sent → Converted → Client. Nothing gets lost — just archived.

⏱

Proposal Age Tracking

A live day counter appears the moment a proposal is sent — colour-coded green, amber, red. Your team always knows who's been waiting too long.

Client Management Built In

Converted leads automatically become Clients with a "Client Since" date. Dedicated client view with tier badges for segmentation and loyalty.

🏷

Membership Tiers

Create custom tier labels — Basic, VIP, or fully custom — with colour-coded badges. Built for businesses with loyalty programs or tiered service offerings.

📊

Sales Team Performance

Leaderboard shows leads, active pipeline, closed won, and close rate per salesperson. Managers get real visibility without chasing reports.

📋

Google Sheet Sync

Already collecting leads via Google Forms? Sync directly into LeadOS with one click. No double entry, no spreadsheet juggling.

📡

Meta Ads Integration

Connect Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns. See which campaigns are generating leads and export your list as CSV anytime.

Role-Based Access

Admins see everything. Sales persons only see their assigned leads. Keeps focus sharp and data clean across your whole team.

Auto-Assign & Round Robin

New leads are distributed automatically across the sales team. No manual allocation — just fair, consistent assignment the moment a lead comes in.
